Book a Party!

private parties!

what event are we looking for?

Avenue Cafe is the perfect place to host a private party or event. The space is available for private hire for a minimum of 50 people (maximum 100). There are two options: room-only hire is £250 with an open bar (excludes food), or a buffet spread from us, where there’s a buffet price, £13.95 per head, but only half the room hire charge (£125.00 when ordering a buffet).

We can provide most genres of background music and it’s possible to have your own DJ/live music. We’re also fully licensed until 11pm (11.30pm finish), so you can enjoy celebrating with a drink into the night. We can apply for a one-hour license extension if required with a Council admin fee. Please email us for further information.
